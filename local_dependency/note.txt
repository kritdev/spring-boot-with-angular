Adding local jar files to a maven project
--------------------------------------
mvn install:install-file -Dfile=[local reporitory path]\font-THSarabunNew.jar -DgroupId=font-THSarabunNew -DartifactId=font-THSarabunNew -Dversion=1.0  -Dpackaging=jar
